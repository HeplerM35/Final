Why You Should Like BTS

Who is BTS?

BTS is a seven member boy group from South Korea under the company Big Hit Entertainment, consisting of members RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Originally gathered as a hiphop group, the group has expanded their musicality through the past six years. Together, they have paved the way for other K-Pop artists in the U.S.

Members

Namjoon Kim, a.k.a RM

Seokjin Kim, a.k.a Jin
RM was the first to join Big Hit Entertainment. He was an underground rapper before auditioning. He is the Leader of BTS and is the Main Rapper and Producer. Jin is the oldest member of BTS. He was casted after being seen getting off of a bus. He originally wanted to be an actor, but after joining Big Hit he became a singer. He is the Visual and Vocalist in BTS.
Yoongi Min, a.k.a Suga Hoseok Jung, a.k.a J-Hope
Suga was an underground rapper. He orginally moved to Seoul to become a Producer, but after he competed in a rapping compatition held by Big Hit and won second place, he joined Big Hit. Suga is the Lead Rapper and Producer of BTS. J-Hope was a street dancer for ten years before auditioning under JYP Entertainment. Unfortunately, the dance group he was in, lost in a "Survival Show." JYP introduced J-Hope to Big Hit and he auditioned. J-Hope then learned how to rap and produce. J-Hope is the Main Dancer, Rapper, Sub-Vocalist, and Producer.
Jimin Park Taehyung Kim, a.k.a V
Jimin was top of his class in contemporary dance at his arts academy. He was the last member to audition and join BTS. Jimin is the Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist, and is known as the 'Face of K-Pop.' V is the son of farmers. He would've been a farmer if Big Hit hadn't noticed him when he joined his friend in auditioning. V is the Vocalist and Lead Dancer in BTS.
Jungkook Jeon
Jungkook was a shy talented kid. He auditioned under several different entertainments but decided to sign his contract for Big Hit Entertainment. He is the Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer, and Sub-Rapper of the group.
