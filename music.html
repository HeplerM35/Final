Why You Should Like BTS

Who is BTS?

BTS is a seven member boy group from South Korea under the company Big Hit Entertainment, consisting of members RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Originally gathered as a hiphop group, the group has expanded their musicality through the past six years. Together, they have paved the way for other K-Pop artists in the U.S.

Members

Namjoon Kim, a.k.a RM

RM was the first to join Big Hit Entertainment. He was an underground rapper before joining. He is the Leader of BTS and is the Main Rapper and Producer.

Seokjin Kim, a.k.a Jin

Jin is the oldest member of BTS. He was casted after being seen getting off of a bus. He originally wanted to be an actor, but after joining Big Hit he became a singer. He is the Visual, Vocalist, and Producer in BTS

Yoongi Min, a.k.a Suga

Suga joined Big Hit after competing in a rapping compatition held by Big Hit. He signed up as an underground rapper. He won second place. He orginally moved to Seoul to become a Producer, but after competing he decided to audition under Big Hit. Suga is the Lead Rapper and Producer of BTS.

Hoseok Jung, a.k.a J-Hope

J-Hope was a street dancer for ten years before auditioning under JYP Entertainment. Unfortunately, the dance group he was in was let go. JYP introduced J-Hope to Big Hit and he auditioned. J-Hope then learned how to rap and produce. J-Hope is the Main Dancer, Rapper, Sub-Vocalist, and Producer.

Jimin Park

Jimin was top of his class in contemporary dance at his arts academy. He was the last member to audition and join BTS. Jimin is the Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist, and is known as the 'Face of K-Pop.'

Taehyung Kim, a.k.a V

V is the son of farmers. He would've been a farmer if Big Hit hadn't noticed him when he joined his friend in auditioning. V is the Vocalist and Lead Dancer in BTS.

Jungkook Jeon

Jungkook was a shy talented kid. He auditioned under big entertainments but denied all of them. He is the Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer, and Sub-Rapper of the group.
